As this is the team’s rookie year, the partnerships it establishes with educational organization and corporate sponsors will serve as a solid foundation for the coming years.An aggressive funding campaign is planned to draw capital from a variety of sources.Between the submission of the proposal and the submission of this document, members of the team have reached out to a variety of organizations on campus. The team has gained the support of the engineering department, and access to a variety of manufacturing resources through partnering with the sustainability lab. Team members are also actively composing a crowdfunding campaign to be hosted on the popular GoFundMe platform. Additional crowd funding will be obtained through participation in Giving Day 2018, which is a unique funding event hosted by the university to fund a variety of student projects.

Rocketry at UCSC is committed to building the most efficient and aerodynamic rocket that will be displayed at the NASA 2017-2018 Student Launch Competition. We are a rookie team that plans to utilize our funds to access top of the line equipment to build the finest rocket. If interested, we would appreciate if you sent us an email. Please donate today!
